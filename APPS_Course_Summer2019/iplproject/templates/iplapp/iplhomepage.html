We love cricket

Cricket is my favorite game. It is a very thrilling game. It is played between two teams consisting of 11 players in each team Indian cricket team is worshipped like a deity in India. Famous cricketers such as Sachin Tendulkar, M.S.Dhoni, Virat Kohli, Sunil Gavaskar, Kapil Dev, etc. are revered as gods. When Indian cricket team plays a match against any other team, the nation comes to a standstill. People leave their work and get glued to TV. If the match happens to be against Pakistan, the entire nation forgets everything except watching the match on TV. The day Indian cricket team wins against the arch-rivals, there is Diwali celebrations throughout the nation. People let off crackers at night to celebrate the victory
